VistA Imaging TeleReader

Starting Work with TeleReader

When the user begins their work session they should launch TeleReader first.  

 

CPRS and VistA Imaging Display will automatically be launched by TeleReader when the user selects a study to view.  

  • CCOW will ensure all applications share the same patient context.  

  • Users may select the sites, specialties, and procedures they will be reading.
