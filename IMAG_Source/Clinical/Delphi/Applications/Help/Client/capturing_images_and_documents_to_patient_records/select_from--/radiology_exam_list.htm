Radiology Exam list

The patient's radiology exams are listed.

Select an entry from the list and click 'OK'

 

Notes:

  • If an exam has images associated with it, an '(I)' will be displayed after the exam status.

  • The widths of the columns can be changed by selecting the column dividers and dragging them to the left or right.

  • There are alternate ways to select an exam from the list
