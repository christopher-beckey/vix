Anatomic Pathology Lab Selection

Selecting a Laboratory Specimen:

 

1 - Click the Select Laboratory Specimen button, located on the left side of the Capture window, just below the patient’s name.

 

2 - The Anatomic Pathology Lab selection window will appear.

3 - Click the Section list (down-arrow), and select a lab section.

4 - Enter the Accession Year and Number to display a list of lab specimens.

 

5 - Click Search. A list of specimens will be displayed.

4 - Select a specimen, then click OK. The Anatomic Pathology Lab selection window will close.

5 - Verify that the left side of the Capture window shows appropriate values for Study Performed and Study Date.

6 - Select a value for the Objective field.

 

7 - Select a value for the Stain field.

8 - Enter a value for the image description.
