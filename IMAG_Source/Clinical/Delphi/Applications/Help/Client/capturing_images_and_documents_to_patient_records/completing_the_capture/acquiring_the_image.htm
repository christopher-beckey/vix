Acquiring the Image

The VistA Imaging Capture Window can use a number of different capture mechanisms to acquire images:

  • Import File from Workstation

  • Scan Document or Color Picture

  • Capturing Meteor Board Still Images

  • Digital and still video camera Input

 

Once an Image has been captured, VistA Imaging will display the image in the Display area and enable the 'Image OK' and 'Cancel' buttons. The image manipulation buttons are also available if required.

 

If the captured image is not the correct image, or not of good quality, click on the 'Cancel' button to discard the image.

 

If the Image is correct and the quality is good, click on the 'Image OK' button.
