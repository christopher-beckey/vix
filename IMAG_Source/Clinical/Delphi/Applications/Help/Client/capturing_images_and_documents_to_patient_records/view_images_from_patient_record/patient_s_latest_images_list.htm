vista imaging capture

Patient's latest images list

Use this list to display images and documents that were captured to the patient's records.

 

On the VistA Imaging Capture main menu - select 'File|Latest Patient Images'.

 

The list is sorted by the Capture Date/Time, with the latest images at the top of the list.

Each image in a Study (Group) is listed individually.

 

Click on an image description, to view the image in the Display Area.

 

Remember:

When displaying images and documents that are already in the patient's record,

  • the Capture button is disabled.

  • To resume capturing images, click the Cancel button.
