Patient Safety Warnings

The Capture software incorporates additional checking to warn users of possible image/patient mismatches. If the following scenario occurs….

 

A. Capture was started from the CPRS Tools menu.

 

B. A patient has been selected and an image is displayed in the image area in the main Capture window, but the capture operation has not yet been completed.

 

C. A different patient is selected in CPRS.
