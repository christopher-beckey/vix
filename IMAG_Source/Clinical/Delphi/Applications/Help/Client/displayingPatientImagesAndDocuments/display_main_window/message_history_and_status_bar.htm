Message history and status bar

At the bottom of the VistA Display and Capture windows is the message/status bar.

 

 

 

During the display and capture process, messages and progress indicators will be displayed.

Click on the  button to open/close the Message history window.

 

 

VistA Imaging maintains a history of messages that have been displayed during the current Imaging session. The messages are accessible by any user.

  • They are intended for use by system managers when problems occur.
