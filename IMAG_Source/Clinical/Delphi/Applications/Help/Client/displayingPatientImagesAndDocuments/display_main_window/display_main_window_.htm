VistA Imaging Display is the main window for viewing patient images and documents.

 

Other windows will be opened during the Display process to:

  • select, manipulate and review information in the patient record.

 

The program provides many options for customizing the Display windows.

  • when these settings are saved, the user's window preferences will be retained on all workstations at that site.

 

1 - Begin by selecting a patient.

  • If a Photo ID of the patient was stored it will be automatically displayed.

  • Patient ID data will be shown just below the patient's name.

  • Image Filter information is displayed beneath the Patient ID data and to the right of the patient's name.

 

2 - The Abstracts window will then open and fill with images and documents -- creating a graphic table of contents to the patient's multi-media record.

 

3 - Click an Abstract - if it represents a group of images, then the Group Abstracts window will fill with images.

 

4 - The Full Resolution View window will fill with numerous medical /administrative images and documents as they are selected.

- Also click: image presentation & navigation, Tips & Tricks

 

5 - Sites with a Marquette MUSE EKG system can elect to display these tracings, using VistA Imaging Display, on all their workstations.

 

6 - The Radiology Viewer window will display multiple radiologic and color (Ophthalmology) images. It provides features for comparison of studies and measurements.

 

7 - There are several windows that display customizable lists of medical information (see: Image Filters) that may also have images and documents attached:

  • Image List

    • can display the image abstract and preview report,

    • while the list is browsed

  • Radiology Exam Listing

  • Progress Notes, Discharge Summaries and Clinical Procedures lists

 

8 - Specialized viewer window.

  • Video Player - window displays motion images.

 

9 - Medical report windows

  • Health Summary

  • Image

  • Patient Profile

  • Discharge Summary
