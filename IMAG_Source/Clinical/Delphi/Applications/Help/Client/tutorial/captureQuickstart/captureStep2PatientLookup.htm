Capture - Step 2: patient lookup

Select the demo patient from VistA.

  • a sample patient used for training

 

Click on the  button (on the upper left side of the window).

 

When the VistA logon window appears enter your Access and Verify codes (these are provided by IRM Staff). See the Reference portion of this manual for additional information about logon.

 

You will now see the Patient Lookup window.

 

In the "Patient" box enter the name of the demo patient at your hospital and press the <Enter> key.

  • If there is more than demo patient name listed,

  • click on the demo patient that you want, then click on the OK button.
