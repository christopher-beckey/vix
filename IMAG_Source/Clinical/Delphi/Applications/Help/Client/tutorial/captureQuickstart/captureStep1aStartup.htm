Capture - Step 1a: Startup

Start VistA Imaging Capture

 

Let's run VistA Imaging Capture

 

Click the START button at the left side of your screen.

  • move your cursor to Programs

  • then move to VistA Imaging Programs

  • then click on VistA Imaging Capture 32bit

 

Users of VistA Imaging Capture must have their Imaging System Manager assign them the VistA user rights and configure their workstation.
