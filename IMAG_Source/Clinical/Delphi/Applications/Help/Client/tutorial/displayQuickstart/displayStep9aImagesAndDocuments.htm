Display - Step 9a: Images and documents

Clinical images and documents, including multiple-page documents, are displayed using the Full Resolution View window.

 

As abstracts are selected from the Abstracts and Group Abstracts windows, they are transferred to the Full Resolution view window.

 

The Layout menu can customize this window.

 

The image with a BLUE selection box is a multi-page document.

The multi-page document control indicates that this MICU Flowsheet is displaying the first of a 15 page document.

  • click the arrows to view the pages consecutively or

  • replace the "1" with an other page number to display
