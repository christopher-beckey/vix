VistA Imaging can display Clinical Procedures images and reports:

  • From the VistA Imaging Display main window:

    • click on the View menu

    • then, click on the Clinical Procedures option

or

  • From the CPRS window

 

The user may sort the procedures by clicking on either 'Title', 'Date', or 'Author' in the heading of the list area.

 

Clicking on an item in the list will cause the display of the images and reports associated with that procedure.
