Radiology Viewer - Magnify Pan window

Use the pan window to zoom in on a small portion of the image.  

  • Click on the ’Magnify’ button on the toolbar  .
  • The button will be depressed, and the small Pan Window will appear next to the radiology window.

 

Use the scrollbar at the bottom right of the magnify window to increase (right) or decrease (left) the magnification.

 

    
  • Place the mouse cursor over the area of the Pan Window to move.
  • Click and drag the mouse down to move the selected area of the image to be shown.
  • The mouse button can be released before moving the mouse.
  • Then, when the mouse is clicked,
    • the magnify window will show the image region near the cusor.
  • Clicking on the button will close the magnify window.
    • The magnify window will also close when the Pan Window is closed.
  • If you click on another image, it will be selected.
    • Clicking again will activate the Magnify function for the newly selected window.
  • In addition to the Magnifying box, you have the option to use a magnifying glass.
  • It will magnify the selected portion directly on the image, where the cursor is placed.
