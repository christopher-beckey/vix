vista imaging display

Scanned documents

Requirement: All document scanning must be at a minimum of 300 x 300 dpi.

Scanned documents are displayed in the Full Resolution View window.

  • multiple documents can be displayed at the same time

 

Documents commonly captured include:

  • consent forms

  • diagrams

  • reports from non-VA facilities

  • 1010EZ

 

 

Notice that the blue highlighted document (example of an Advance Directive) is a multi-page document image. The multi-page controls on the upper right portion of the window are enabled. The control window indicates that page 1 of the 3 page document is currently displayed.
