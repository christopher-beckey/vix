This window has many features:

o     it shows multiple images simultaneously, in full color and/or black/white

o     it displays documents

o     it enables user manipulation of => single images, all images or selected groups of images

·      load the window with an assortment of images (medical and documents)

·      click the Apply actions to all images' button and adjust their size.

·      hold the Ctrl key and click on all of the documents.

·      with only the documents selected, zoom the slider until the text is readable.

·      results - review medical images while simultaneously reading supporting documents.

 

Display features:

o     Use the Brightness (the balance of light and dark shades), Contrast (the range between the lightest and darkest tones) and Zoom sliders to adjust the brightness, contrast and size of the image as it appears on the screen.

·      Note: Changing the display does not affect the recorded image.

o     Image proxies are available in the Abstracts, Group Abstracts and Full Resolution View windows. Proxies are displayed as a white rectangle.

o     Images selected from other procedures or groups are added to the Full Resolution View window, they do not replace any of the currently displayed images.

o     Single images or groups can be selected and removed from the display window, at any time.
