Loading abstracts

This window will display when a page of abstracts is being loaded in either the Abstracts or Group Abstracts window.

 

 

This window shows which abstracts are being loaded (in this case, the first 24)

  • also, which one is currently being loaded (#7).

 

Clicking the 'Cancel' button will stop the loading of abstracts after the current abstract is loaded.

 

Note: If you click on an abstract, in the Abstracts window, while the abstracts are loading

  • the Image will be displayed,

  • then, the loading of abstracts will continue.
