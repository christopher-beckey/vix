Patient Lookup

The Patient Lookup window lists the patient names that match the characters typed in the Patient edit box.

 

Part of the patient’s name, the full Social Security Number, or the patient’s last name initial and last four digits of the patient’s Social Security Number can be entered. If multiple patients are found, select one from the list and click on the 'OK' button.

 

 

Note: The Patient Alerts window will display any Restricted Patient, Similar Patient Record or Means Test Required alerts.

 

Notes:

  • VistA Imaging displays patient demographic information for the selected patient on the information panel below the Patient Selection text box. This includes:Social Security Number, Date of Birth, Service Connected status, and Type of patient.

  • There are alternate ways to select a list entry.

  • The font of the patient list can be changed by clicking the Font button.

  • The patient selected will be inserted into the recent patients list, on the 'File' menu.
