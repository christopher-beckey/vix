Administrative Documents

Administrative documents can be scanned using the Administrative association.

  • Select the Patient whose document will be scanned by clicking the 'Select Patient' button.

  • Select the type of administrative document to be scanned from the list. For example, MEANS TEST may be selected. Scanned MEANS TESTS will be automatically copied to the Health Eligibility Center to comply with VA Directive 2001-073.

  • Enter the document's date such as 12/14/2001 or T for today’s date.

 

The Image Description will say MEANS TEST (10-10EZ) or whatever Document type was selected from the pull-down list. The description may be edited, but probably will not need to be.

 

  • Place MEANS TEST document on scanner.

  • Click on the capture button to begin scanning.

  • Check the 'Multi Pg.ADF' box if you wish to use the automatic document feeder on your scanner. Leave this box unchecked if you will be putting the page on the glass platen.

  • Click on the capture button to begin scanning.

 

 

You will hear the scanner working.  The scanned image will appear (after a short period of time) in the white window on the right side.

 

You will be asked to confirm the save settings.

  • Click on the OK button.

 

When the capture process is completed, the scanned document will disappear from the right-hand window and you will be able to scan the next document.

 

The image will now be available for viewing on the clinical workstation.  It can also be viewed from the capture workstation by selecting the File option and then 'Patient Latest Images'.
