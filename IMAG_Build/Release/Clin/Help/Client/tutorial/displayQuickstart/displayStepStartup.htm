Display - Step 1: Startup

Start VistA Imaging Display

Let's run VistA Imaging Display.

 

 

Click the START button at the left side of your screen.

  • move your cursor to Programs

  • then move to VistA Imaging Programs

  • then click on VistA Imaging Display 32bit

 

Your Imaging System Manager will configure VistA Imaging Display to appear before or after you logon to your VistA system.

 

If the VistA logon window appears then enter your Access and Verify codes (these are provided by your IRM Service).
