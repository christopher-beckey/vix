Display - Step 9b: Images and documents

Select and de-select single and groups of images.

  • hold down the CTRL key (and continue to do so, asked to release it).

  • move the cursor over the MICU Flowsheet and click on the image.

  • the Flowsheet is de-selected.

  • move the cursor over an image (of a foot) and click on the image.

  • the image is selected (a BLUE box appears)

  • move the cursor over another image (of a foot) and click on the image.

  • the image is selected (a BLUE box appears around this image also).

  • release the CTRL key.

 

Move the cursor over the Fit to Width button and click it.

  • both selected images are now expanded to fit the width of their image boxes.
